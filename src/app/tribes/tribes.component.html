Tribes of Tanzania

Maasai Tribe

The Maasai tribe is one of the most well-known ethnic groups in Tanzania. They are known for their vibrant red attire, distinct jewelry, and traditional jumping dance. The Maasai are semi-nomadic pastoralists, renowned for their rich cultural heritage and strong sense of community.

Chagga Tribe

The Chagga tribe is primarily found around the slopes of Mount Kilimanjaro. They are known for their agricultural practices, including coffee and banana farming. The Chagga people have a rich history, unique language, and traditional practices, making them an important part of Tanzanian culture.
